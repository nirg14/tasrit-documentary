# Tasrit Documentary Project

## Project Overview

This is a comprehensive Holocaust survivor testimony documentary project based on the testimony of a Romanian Jewish survivor. The documentary is organized into 48 video segments (סרטון), chronicling the journey from pre-war Romania through the Holocaust period and eventual immigration to Israel in 1948.

## Documentary Timeline

### Part 1: Pre-War Romania (Segments 1-5)
- Life in Iași and Bucharest
- Family background
- Rising antisemitism
- Early childhood memories

### Part 2: War Years (Segments 6-12)
- The Iași pogrom (1941)
- Forced labor camps
- Living in hiding
- Daily life under persecution

### Part 3: Post-War Displacement (Segments 13-35)
- Decision to leave Romania (1947)
- Journey through:
  - Transylvania → Hungary → Austria → Germany
- DP camps:
  - Vienna (Rothschild Hospital, Herzberger School)
  - Linz, Austria
  - Bad Reichenhall, Germany
  - Eschwege, Germany

### Part 4: Journey to Israel (Segments 36-48)
- Displaced Persons camps in Germany
- 1948 Israeli Independence
- Immigration to Israel
- Arrival and settling in the new country
