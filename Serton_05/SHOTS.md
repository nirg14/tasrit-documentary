**Setting:** Bucharest attic tower, Matei Basarab square below, early 1940s
**Emotional Tone:** Terror, helplessness, witnessing horror, traumatic memory
**Key Action:** Child watches from attic window as Jews are rounded up and loaded onto buses for deportation
