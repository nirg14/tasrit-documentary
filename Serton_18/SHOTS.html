Serton 18: Exhausted Sleep

Szeged, Hungary, Jewish orphanage, 1947

Setting

Szeged, Hungary, Jewish orphanage, 1947

Emotional Tone

Utter exhaustion, relief, deep sleep, loss of time

Key Action

Child sleeps 12 hours straight, losing all sense of time after grueling escape journey

Shot Count

3 shots total

Emotional Arc

Opens with complete physical collapse from exhaustion, moves through timeless deep sleep that transcends normal rest, concludes with disoriented awakening that reveals the depth of physical and psychological exhaustion - thinking only two hours passed when twelve have gone. This moment encapsulates the toll of the escape journey.

Historical Context

Jewish orphanages in Hungary 1947 were way-stations for refugees fleeing Eastern Europe, providing basic shelter to those en route to DP camps in Austria and Germany.
