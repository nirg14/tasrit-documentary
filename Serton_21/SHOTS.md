**Emotional Arc:** Opens with uncertain arrival of Soviet vehicle, reveals Jewish officer who recognizes the plight, exposing Austrian guards' lie about zones, courageously confronts guards and protects refugees. Moment of miraculous intervention where someone with power and moral courage appears at darkest moment. Shows how individual acts of bravery could save lives.

**Historical Context:** Some Soviet Jewish officers who had survived or whose families died in Holocaust, sometimes helped Jewish refugees when they encountered them. Austria 1947 was divided into zones but corrupt border guards would sometimes claim to be in Russian zone to extort refugees or avoid processing them.
