Serton 19: Rothschild Horror

Vienna, Rothschild Hospital DP camp courtyard, 1947

Setting

Vienna, Rothschild Hospital DP camp courtyard, 1947

Emotional Tone

Shock, degradation, horror at conditions, loss of dignity

Key Action

Family arrives at overcrowded refugee camp, witnesses unsanitary conditions, decides not to enter

Shot Count

6 shots total

Emotional Arc

Family approaches with hope for shelter, experiences crushing revelation of horrific overcrowding and unsanitary conditions, witnesses systematic degradation of human beings, makes protective decision to preserve dignity by refusing to enter despite desperate need. Shows that even after Holocaust, survivors faced dehumanizing treatment.

Historical Context

Rothschild Hospital Vienna 1947 was massively overcrowded DP camp with thousands of Jewish refugees. Conditions were documented as deplorable, with inadequate sanitation, overcrowding, disease, and loss of privacy and dignity.
