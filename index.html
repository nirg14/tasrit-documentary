Project Overview

This documentary tells the story of a Holocaust survivor's journey from Romania through post-war Europe, animated in 48 distinct segments (Sertons). Each Serton is broken down into multiple shots, with detailed Midjourney prompts for both static images and video animation.

Historical Timeline

1940-1944: Childhood in Romania

Sertons 1-10: Early life, Yellow Star decree, ghetto experiences

1944: Deportation

Sertons 11-15: Selection, train journey, Auschwitz arrival

1944-1945: Survival

Sertons 16-30: Camp experiences, liberation, displacement

1945-1947: Journey to Freedom

Sertons 31-48: Border crossings, DP camps, search for new life

Visual Style Guide

Visual Style

  • 2D animated historical illustration
  • Moving painting aesthetic
  • Serious, evocative, melancholic tone

Color Palette

  • Heavily desaturated sepia tones
  • Muted browns and deep greys
  • Almost monochromatic presentation

Lighting

  • High-contrast chiaroscuro
  • Long oppressive shadows
  • Dramatic period-appropriate lighting
