## Documentary Structure

### Act I: Pre-War and War Years (Segments 1-12)
**Location:** Romania (Iași, Bucharest)
**Timeline:** 1935-1946
**Key Themes:**
- Family life before the war
- Rising antisemitism
- Father's forced labor
- Iași pogrom and family losses
- Liberation and post-war chaos

### Act II: Escape Journey (Segments 13-35)
**Route:** Romania → Hungary → Austria → Germany
**Timeline:** 1947-1948
**Key Themes:**
- Decision to leave
- Border crossings and smugglers
- DP camps across Europe
- Waiting for immigration papers
- Declaration of Israeli independence

### Act III: Journey to Israel (Segments 36-48)
**Route:** Germany → Mediterranean → Israel
**Timeline:** 1948
**Key Themes:**
- Final preparations
- Sea voyage
- Arrival in the homeland
- Beginning new life
- Reflections on survival

## How to Use This Project

### For Production Team

1. **Pre-Production Phase**
   - Review each segment's README.md
   - Collect required images from checklist
   - Create required maps
   - Record voiceover scripts

2. **Production Phase**
   - Follow segment order (1-48)
   - Check off items in production_notes.md
   - Place assets in respective images/ folders
   - Create animations

3. **Post-Production Phase**
   - Review completed segments
   - Ensure continuity between segments
   - Final color grading and audio mix
   - Export and archive

### For Researchers

- Each segment includes historical context
- Locations are documented
- Timeline is preserved
- Personal testimony is preserved verbatim

### For Translators

- Original Hebrew text in script.txt
- Context provided in README.md
- Cultural references noted
